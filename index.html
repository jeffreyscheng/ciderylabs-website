Cidery Labs is an independent AI research lab. We are pursuing a small number of ideas we believe matter enormously, at the pace they deserve.

Research

We work at the frontier of what learning could become. Our research asks the questions beneath the questions — not how models train, but what training wants to be.

We are not ready to say more. When we are, you will know.

Approach

  • Depth over noise. We believe the important problems are quiet. We sit with them.
  • Small, by design. A handful of people, thinking carefully, for as long as it takes.
  • Patience as strategy. The future does not need to be rushed. It needs to be understood.
